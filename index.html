Got Questions? We've Got Answers!

How to report a pothole?

Reporting a pothole is as easy as snapping a photo and submitting it through our user-friendly platform!

What happens after I report?

Once you report a pothole, our team of officials swing into action, prioritizing repairs based on the severity of the issue!

Is my information safe?

Absolutely! Your data is confidential and only used by officials to improve road conditions and safety!

How can I track the repair progress?

You can track the repair status in real-time on our platform, ensuring you stay in the loop every step of the way!
